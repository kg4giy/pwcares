Consolidated Training Requirements for 2014

The initial four course are required by both the Commonwealth of Virginia, as well as Prince William County. You only need to take the course once for each major number. Upon completion of any of these courses, please send a copy of the certificate to the Emergency Coordinator for the files. Additional course may be added as available or required.

Federal/County/City Requirements

All members
  • IS-700 National Incident Management System (NIMS)
  • IS-100.B Incident Command System
  • IS-200.B ICS for Single Resources and Initial Action Incidents
  • IS-800.B National Response Framework
Optional
  • G-191 Incident Command System/Emergency Operations Center Interface. Classroom based. Dates announced as availble.
  • IS-701.a NIMS Multiagency Coordination System (MACS) Course
  • IS-706 NIMS Intrastate Mutual Aid - An Introduction
  • IS-775: EOC Management and Operations
  • G-775 Emergency Operations Center (EOC) Management and Operations. Classroom based. Dates announced as available.
  • IS-802 ESF #2
Optional (Leadership)
  • ICS-300 Intermediate ICS. Classroom based. Dates announced as available.
  • ICS-400 Advanced ICS. Classroom based. Dates announced as available.
  • IS-775, EOC Management and Operations
