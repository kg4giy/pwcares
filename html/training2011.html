Consolidated Training Requirements for 2011

Federal/County Requirements

All members
  • IS-700 National Incident Management System (NIMS)
  • IS-100 Incident Command System
  • IS-200 ICS for Single Resources and Initial Action Incidents
  • IS-800 National Response Framework
Optional
  • IS-802 ESF #2
Optional (Leadership)
  • ICS-300 Intermediate ICS. Classroom based.
  • ICS-400 Advanced ICS. Classroom based.

Along with the FEMA certifications, each ARES member is expected to attend at least three training sessions per year.

The following have already been approved as training sessions and more will be added as events occur throughout the year.

Please inform the EC if you attend one of these sessions or wish to have an event approved as a training session.
