Rally Point

We will meet on Saturday, December 9, 2017 at 1600 at the Manassas Park Police Department, 329 Manassas Drive, Manassas Park, Virginia, behind the 7-Eleven. Pizza and drinks will be available.

Mission

Our mission is to Provide Safety

  • Keep vehicles and people separated
  • Crowd and Traffic Management
  • Extra set of eyes

A detailed presentation was done on Wednesday, December 6, 2017. If you missed it, you can view it here.

Equipment

Please dress for the weather. Current predictions are for afternoon sun, light winds from the south east and 49% humidity with a high of 40 degrees and a low of 19 degrees.

Reflective vests will be provided if you do not have your own. Please bring a flashlight with a traffic wand if you have one. If you have electronic flares you may also find them useful.

CERT members can use their FRS radios (Channel 2) or if they are licensed, they can use the Amateur Radio frequency. Amateurs will use OVH2M (146.970- PL 100.0 Hz). You may find a headset and microphone useful.

Steve, N4OGR, and Jan, KE4TMW, will be our Net Control for both frequencies.

Media Relations

Please direct any media inquiries to the Manassas Park Police Department. We will have Tom, W4PIO, with us for those who have questions about Amateur Radio.
