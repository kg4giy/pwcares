Mission

Our primary mission is eyes on the course to provide safety. In the event of an emergency, please dial 9–1–1, then report the incident to Shadow.

There is no swim in this event, and the bike route is different than it has been in the past. Please review the maps to ensure you are familiar with the changes. The Bike race is a three loop course. The entire bike course is to be raced 3 times. At the end of the 3rd loop, duathletes are to dismount and enter the transition area. There are two runs, one before and one after the bike portion and the runs are into the woods.

Equipment

HTs should be sufficient for this event, mobiles are also acceptable. Shadow will likely require headsets due to the amount of noise in the area.

Operational Parameters

We will plan to operate on simplex at 147.525. We will backstop this with the the W4OVH repeater at 146.970- PL 100.0.
