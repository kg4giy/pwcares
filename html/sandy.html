Skywarn is active on 147.300

About 6pm tonight the WA4TSC repeater lost power and is on battery backup. I am not sure how long the repeater can last on battery, but the weather eagle was turned off to save power and it only says "On Battery" after every 4th transmission.

In order to maximize the battery life, I recommend that we start to phase in the two backup repeaters. WX4LWX can monitor all three frequencies and we will be making the announcements on all 3 frequencies.

Our primary backup repeaters are either 145.21- pl 141.3 (K4QJZ Linden, VA) or 146.955- (KV3B Rockville, MD). Stations are requested to QSY to the most readable primary net frequency or to the best available subnet frequency.
